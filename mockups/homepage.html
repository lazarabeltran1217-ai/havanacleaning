Our Story

Born in Havana,
Built in Miami.

Havana Cleaning was founded by two sisters who brought their Cuban heritage — and their grandmother's relentless standards for cleanliness — to every home they touch.

We're not just a cleaning company. We're a family business with deep roots, and we bring that warmth and dedication into every space we clean. Every product we use is eco-friendly, every technician is trained and vetted, and every clean comes with our personal guarantee.

From Kendall to Coral Gables, from Brickell condos to Hialeah family homes — we bring the same Cuban pride to every address.
